Fruit Bowl Recipe

Description

Fruit bowls are very nice! With enough fruits, you get a good variety of flavors and textures all in one bowl. This recipe is my favorite way of making a fruit bowl, containing all my favorite fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 1 mango
  • 2 bananas
  • 1/2 pineapple

Steps

  1. Wash the berries, and pour them directly into a large bowl.
  2. Cut the mango in half and remove the seed, then dice the mango into medium sized cubes.
  3. Peel the bananas and cut them into thin slices.
  4. Cut the pineapple in half and dice the pineapple.
  5. Add the banana, mango and pineapple to the bowl and mix the fruits by shaking the bowl.
  6. Serve in a glass cup or enjoy it directly from the bowl!
