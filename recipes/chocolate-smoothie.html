Chocolate Smoothie Recipe

Description

Who doesn't love chocolate smoothies!? I sure do!

You can add all your favorite toppings and ingredients to it, and this recipe shows how to make an excellent smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups natural yoghurt
  • 3 tbps cocoa powder
  • 1 tbps sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Pour the milk and yoghurt into a blender, and blend it until smooth.
  2. Add the cocoa powder, sugar and vanilla extract and keep blending it.
  3. Crack the egg into a bowl, separate the egg yolk from the egg white.
  4. Pour the contents of the blender into a separate container and put the egg white into the blender.
  5. Blend the egg white until fluffy and then pour the rest of the contents back into the blender along with the egg yolk, and keep blending.
  6. Once it is blended and smooth in texture, pour into a cup and enjoy your chocolate smoothie!
